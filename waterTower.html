Water Tower Place

About:

Water Tower Place is a large urban, mixed-use development comprising a 758,000 sq ft shopping mall, hotel, theater, and condominiums in a 74-story skyscraper in Chicago, Illinois, United States. The mall is located at 835 North Michigan Avenue, along the Magnificent Mile.
