How to Ride with Uber and Lyft:


Steps to Ride with Uber:
  1. Download the Uber app and make an account
  2. Select the "Ride" option
  3. Enter your current location and destination
  4. Select your ride (UberX, UberXL, Uber Black, Uber Cab, etc.)
  5. Tap 'Confirm' to shecdule your ride!

Steps to Ride with Lyft:
  1. Download the Lyft app and make an account
  2. Tap 'Search Destination' and enter your destination
  3. Choose your date and time and tap 'Set pickup time'
  4. Confirm your pickup location and dropoff location
  5. Tap 'Schedule' to schedule your ride!
