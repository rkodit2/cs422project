Parking




General Parking Rules for Chicago

  • Don't park more than 12 in from the curb
  • Don't park in bus or bike lanes; Those are used by busses and bikers daily
  • Park at least 20 ft away from stop signs
  • Park at least 15 ft away from fire hydrants
  • Make sure you display your city sticker on the lower right hand of your windshield
  • Never park in the direction opposite to the flow of traffic
  • Never park on a sidewalk, parkway, or crosswalk
