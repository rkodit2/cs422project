Millenium Park

About:

Millennium Park is a public park located in the Loop community area of Chicago in Illinois operated by the Chicago Department of Cultural Affairs and managed by MB Real Estate.

Costs:

Millenium Park is free for all to enjoy!


Covid Guidelines:

Covid Guidelines are set by the CDC as followed:
Visitors who are vaccinated are not required to wear masks
Visitors who are unvaccinated are required to wear face masks in outdoor and crowded environments

Location:
