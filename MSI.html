Museum of Science and Industry

About:

Founded by Julius Rosenwald and first opened in 1933, Chicago's Museum of Science and Industry is one of the largest science museums in the world. MSI works to inspire and motivate visitors of all ages, and fuel an interest in Science, Engineering, Medicine, and Technology with its interactive experiences and exhibits.
