Sears Tower

About:

The 12th tallest tower in the world, Sears Tower stands at 1,450 feet, 110 stories tall, and is also the highest public viewing area throughout the US.

Costs:

Costs vary on a day by day basis:
Adult (Ages 12+): $28.00 - $35.00
Youth (Ages 3-11): $22.00 - $26.00
Children under 3 are free!

Covid Guidelines:

All visitors are required to wear face masks regardless of vaccination status

Location:

Hours of Operation:
9 AM - 10 PM Monday through Sunday
