SpotHero


About:

SpotHero is a digital parking marketplace that allows users to reserve and pay for parking spaces in lots, garages, and valet servcies


How To Use SpotHero:


How to use the SpotHero Website:
  1. Select your type of parking from Hourly, Monthly, and Airport
  2. Enter your location, or select "Find Parking Near Me"
  3. Add any necessary information, like enter and exit time
  4. Scroll through and find the best parking option for you, and select "Book Now"
  5. Enter your email and continue as a guest, or sign in or create and acount for reservation details
  6. Review your reservation details, and enter in your phone number, along with the make and model of your car
  7. Enter your payment method, and to finish, tap "Pay and Reserve"
  8. All details and passes for your reservation will be emailed to you, so scan the provided QR code while entering and exiting the parking area for access
Link to SpotHero Main Website

How to use the SpotHero App:
  1. Sign in, Create and account, or continue as a guest
  2. Enter your Address, Venue, or Airport
  3. Select your type of parking from Hourly, Monthly, and Airport
  4. Enter the start and end times for parking
  5. Scroll through and select bubbles to find the best parking option for you, and select "Book Now"
  6. Select "Checkout" to continue to payment
  7. Review your reservation details, and enter in your phone number, along with the make and model of your car
  8. Enter your payment method, and to finish, tap "Reserve Spot"
  9. To access your reservation, open up the "Reservations" Tab and select your reservation, and scan the QR code while entering and exiting the parking area for access
