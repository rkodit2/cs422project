Divvy Bikes

About:

Divvy is a bike sharing system created by Lyft, with several stations dispered around the City of Chicago, so you can pick up and drop off your bike with ease!

Costs:

Single Ride: $3.30/Ride (One ride up to 30 minutes)
Day Pass: $15/Day (Unlimited 3 hour rides within a 24 hour period)
Annual Pass: $9/Month (Unlimited 45 minute rides)


How to Ride with Divvy:

  1. Buy a ride pass from a Divvy station kiosk or by downloading the Divvy Bikes App
  2. Find an available bike station nearby your location
  3. Scan your QR code or use your member key to unlock a bike
  4. Ride as long as you pass or membership is active
  5. After your pass or membership is inactive, return your bike to any avaiable station, and wait for the bike to lock in, indicated by the green light on the dock
