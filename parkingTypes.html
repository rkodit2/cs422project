Types of Parking


About:

The main parking types for tourists in Chicago are: meter, paid and public



Meter

  • Those are parkings on the street
  • Look for green "Pay to Park" or "P" sign and small pay machine
  • You have to pay for how many hours you want your car to be parked for
  • Since parking in Chicago is modern, cash as well as credit and debit cards are accepted
  • Rates will vary depending on day and time


Paid

  • Paid parkings could be hotels, garages or even attraction's on site parkings
  • Usually a bit more protected, not on street
  • Rates can vary depending on where and what kind of paid parking you get


Public

  • Public ones are the free parkings offered to all
  • Usually they are located near public parks and other public areas
  • Be careful, don't count on having enough space to park your car, usually most spots are taken
